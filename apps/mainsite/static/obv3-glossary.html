Edubadges OpenBadge version 3.0 Glossary

This document provides definitions for terms used in the Edubadges OpenBadge version 3.0 specification. It explains the extensions and differences with the published OpenBadge Specification.

BadgeClass

A type of badge, with different business rules and attributes for each type.

RegularAchievement

A badge class for acquired knowledge and skills that are part of the curriculum of a recognized education program. This badge class is associated with ECTS/SBU.

ECTS
European Credit Transfer and Accumulation System
Time Investment
The time investment required to recieve the credential. In hours, and not associated with either the SBU or ECTS framework.
SBU
Study Burden Unit

MicroCredentialAchievement

A badge class for microcredentials with the quality framework for professionals HBO/WO or MBO and all metadata conform the EU recommendations.

ExtraCurricularAchievement

A badge class for acquired knowledge and skills that are not part of the curriculum of a recognized education program. This badge class is not associated with ECTS/SBU.

EducredentialAchievement

An Achievement that matches the OpenBadge v3 specification and defines the extensions for all of the RegularAchievement, MicroCredentialAchievement and ExtraCurricularAchievement badge classes.
It extends the [OpenBadge v3 Achievement](https://www.imsglobal.org/spec/ob/v3p0/#achievement). It uses the extension ability as defined in the spec and as suggested to be implemented in the [OpenBadge impl guidelines](https://www.imsglobal.org/spec/ob/v3p0/impl/#open-badges-extensions)

AssessmentType
The type of assessment used to determine if the badge class has been achieved.
IdentityChecked
Indicates if the identity of the badge earner has been checked.
SupervisionType
The type of supervision required during assessement.
ParticipationType
The type of participation required to achieve the badge class.
Testing
Assessment by testing the knowledge and skills of the badge earner.
Application of a skill
Assessment by applying a skill in a real-life situation.
Portfolio
Assessment by presenting a portfolio of work.
Recognition of prior learning
Assessment by recognizing prior learning.
Unsupervised with no identity verification
Assessment without supervision and without identity verification.
Supervised with no identity verification
Assessment with supervision but without identity verification.
Supervised online
Assessment with supervision online.
Onsite with identity verification
Assessment with supervision onsite and with identity verification.
Online
Participation online.
Onsite or blended
Participation onsite or online or both.
Volunteering
Participation by volunteering.
Work experience
Participation by work experience.
Personalized learning activities
Participation by personalized learning activities.
EducationProgramIdentifier
The identifier of the education program.
